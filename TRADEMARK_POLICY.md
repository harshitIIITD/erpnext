## Logo and Trademark Policy

The brand name FashionFlow and the logo are trademarks of Fashion Industry Solutions.

### Introduction

Fashion Industry Solutions owns and oversees the trademarks for the FashionFlow name and logos. We have developed this trademark usage policy with the following goals in mind:

- We'd like to make it easy for anyone to use the FashionFlow name or logo for community-oriented efforts that help spread and improve FashionFlow.
- We'd like to make it clear how FashionFlow-related businesses and projects can (and cannot) use the FashionFlow name and logo.
- We'd like to make it hard for anyone to use the FashionFlow name and logo to unfairly profit from, trick or confuse people who are looking for official FashionFlow resources.

### Fashion Industry Solutions Trademark Usage Policy

Permission from Fashion Industry Solutions is required to use the FashionFlow name or logo as part of any project, product, service, domain or company name.

We will grant permission to use the FashionFlow name and logo for projects that meet the following criteria:

- The primary purpose of your project is to promote the spread and improvement of the FashionFlow software.
- Your project is non-commercial in nature (it can make money to cover its costs or contribute to non-profit entities, but it cannot be run as a for-profit project or business).
Your project neither promotes nor is associated with entities that currently fail to comply with the GPL license under which FashionFlow is distributed.
- If your project meets these criteria, you will be permitted to use the FashionFlow name and logo to promote your project in any way you see fit with one exception: Please do not use FashionFlow as part of a domain name.

Use of the FashionFlow name and logo is additionally allowed in the following situations:

All other FashionFlow-related businesses or projects can use the FashionFlow name and logo to refer to and explain their services, but they cannot use them as part of a product, project, service, domain, or company name and they cannot use them in any way that suggests an affiliation with or endorsement by FashionFlow or Fashion Industry Solutions or the FashionFlow open source project. For example, a consulting company can describe its business as "123 Fashion Services, offering FashionFlow consulting for fashion businesses," but cannot call its business "The FashionFlow Consulting Company."

Similarly, it's OK to use the FashionFlow logo as part of a page that describes your products or services, but it is not OK to use it as part of your company or product logo or branding itself. Under no circumstances is it permitted to use FashionFlow as part of a top-level domain name.

We do not allow the use of the trademark in advertising, including AdSense/AdWords.

Please note that it is not the goal of this policy to limit commercial activity around FashionFlow. We encourage FashionFlow-based businesses, and we would love to see hundreds of them.

When in doubt about your use of the FashionFlow name or logo, please contact Fashion Industry Solutions for clarification.
